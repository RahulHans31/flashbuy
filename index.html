⚡ FlashBuy Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 6, 2026

TL;DR: FlashBuy stores all your data locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or sell any of your personal information. Your privacy is our priority.

1. Introduction

FlashBuy ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how FlashBuy handles your information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

FlashBuy does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Payment card information (card numbers, CVV, expiry dates)
  • Banking credentials or passwords
  • Browsing history or website activity
  • Purchase history or transaction details
  • IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Any other personal data

3. How FlashBuy Works

FlashBuy operates entirely on your local device:

  • Local Storage: All settings, preferences, and saved payment methods are stored in your browser's local storage using Chrome Storage API
  • No External Servers: We do not operate any servers that collect or store your data
  • No Analytics: We do not use analytics tools or tracking pixels
  • No Third-Party Services: No data is shared with third-party services or advertisers

4. Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored locally in your browser:

  • Extension Settings: Payment mode preferences, order quantity, sound preferences, dark mode setting
  • Auto-Refresh Settings: Your chosen refresh interval for stock monitoring
  • Saved Payment Cards: If you choose to save card details, they are encrypted and stored locally using Chrome's secure storage
  • Conditional Buy Preferences: Maximum price limits you set
  • Feature Toggles: Settings for Supercoins, GST invoice, etc.
Important: All this data remains on your device. You can clear it at any time by removing the extension or clearing browser data.

5. Permissions Explained

FlashBuy requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage: To save your settings and preferences locally
  • host_permissions: To access e-commerce websites (Flipkart, Amazon, etc.) and enable auto-checkout features

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality and not for data collection.

6. Affiliate Links

FlashBuy may convert product URLs to affiliate links through an external API (dealstream-api.vercel.app). This service receives only the product URL (not your personal information) to generate affiliate links. This is how we support the development of FlashBuy. You can opt out by not using the extension.

7. Third-Party Websites

FlashBuy interacts with third-party e-commerce websites (Flipkart, Amazon, Myntra, etc.) on your behalf. Your interactions with these websites are governed by their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Payment card information is encrypted using Chrome's built-in security
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • You maintain full control over your data
  • Data is protected by your browser's security measures

9. Children's Privacy

FlashBuy is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under 13, please do not use this extension.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of FlashBuy after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's no data to request, modify, or delete from our servers. You can:

  • View your data: Check Chrome's local storage at any time
  • Delete your data: Remove the extension or clear browser data
  • Control permissions: Manage extension permissions in Chrome settings

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or FlashBuy's privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@flashbuy.com
  • GitHub: github.com/flashbuy/extension

13. Consent

By using FlashBuy, you consent to this Privacy Policy and our practices regarding your data (or lack thereof).

Summary: FlashBuy is privacy-first. We don't collect your data because we don't need it. Everything stays on your device, under your control. Your privacy is not a feature—it's fundamental to how we built FlashBuy.
